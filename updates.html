The following are updates from MCO.

Moriah Discipleship Graduation



There will be a graduation on the 16th June 2017 at Moriah School of Discipleship in Maseno, Kisumu County. The graduation will be the 5th since its inception and will run from 9am to 12.30pm.

Feeding Program Partners



MCO is looking for partners to raise foodstuffs, like dry maize, beans, rice, sugar for our feeding program. Please contact us if you are willing to partner with us.

The SFL Association



MCO is spearheading the formation of an independent association of all the SFL affiliate groups for investment and training.
