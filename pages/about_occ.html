  1. About Outreach Community Centre (OCC)

OUTREACH COMMUNITY CENTRE hereunto referred as the "Church" as was constituted in April 1997 being an evangelical Christian outreach church to bringing all Christians of similar faith or belief as prescribed in the Statement of Faith. The head office is situated in Nairobi City. The church currently have branches in the following counties,Nairobi,Machakos,Kisumu,Homa bay, Migori and Siaya. We, the church, the called out body of Jesus Christ desiring that all things be done in a fitting and orderly manner as referred to in 1 Corinthians 14:40, we solemnly and sincerely, adopt, subscribes, enact this constitution by which we voluntarily agree to be governed as a church both for ourselves and the future churches that shall be planted.

Our Purpose

  • To preach reach each with the holistic gospel of Jesus Christ.

  • To worship the Triune God, seeking to respond in obedience to His leadership, according to the Scriptures.
