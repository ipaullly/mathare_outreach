PART TIME TEACHER NEEDED



KE 355 Mathare outreach Child Development Centre is a Compassion International assisted Project that seeks to release Mathare Children and Youth from Spiritual, Physical, Social and Economic Poverty in Jesus name. We are looking for the best Persons to fill the following position:

Desired Qualifications


  • He/She MUST Confess Jesus Christ as saviour and possess firm relationship and rooting in the Christian ideals of Integrity, Stewardship, Excellence and dignity to fellow human being.
  • He/She has as Calling and heart desire to work in a ministry of Babies, Children and Youth of ages 0-22 years in a Church Environment.
  • MUST be a Team Player in finding solutions towards the Orphans and Vulnerable Babies, Children and Youth.
  • He/She MUST Demonstrate Commitment to work with minimal supervision and willing to work extra hours as deemed fit by the management.
  • He/She MUST possess at least a Diploma early child hood education( ECD ) OR equivalent certification. Training in Holistic Child and Youth Development would be an added advantage.
  • Flexible to assume any duty relating to Children and Youth Development as and when given to execute
  • He/ She SHOULD have at least 2years work experience in the same role.
  • MUST be available on Saturday.


Application should be done before 28th July 2017


Qualified Persons to Send and Address their Detailed updated Curriculum Vitae and Cover letter with official Contact information (Phone number and Email), Scanned Copies of Pastor’s recommendation letter not older than 3 months to The CPC Chairman, CDC KE 355 MATHARE OUTREACH .
