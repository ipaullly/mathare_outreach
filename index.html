Our Centres

Mathare Community Outreach (MCO)

MCO is a grassroot interdenominational community development initiative dedicated to improving the lives of the disenfranchised children and their families in Mathare Valley slums.
It started in 1994 as a feeding programme where children were fed on a cup of porridge a day following increased malnutrition and related diseases among the children

Outreach Community Centre (OCC)

OCC was constituted in April 1997 being an evangelical Christian outreach church to bringing all Christians of similar faith or belief as prescribed in the Statement of Faith.
The head office is situated in Nairobi City. The church currently have branches in the following counties, Nairobi, Machakos, Kisumu, Homa bay, Migori and Siaya.

Kariobangi Outreach Children's Home

The Children's center was started in the year 1997 with an objective to care and support Orphans & Vulnerable Children largely affected by HIV/AIDS scourge.
Managed by Outreach Community Center, the center caters for children aged 4 years to 18 years. The home is located in Gitathuru area of Kariobangi North.
